What is Agile?

AgileManifesto.org

Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
Working software over comprehensive documentation
Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
Responding to change over following a plan

Why Agile?

  • Happier, more engaged, smarter customer through greater collaboration
  • Less waste - Discover whats really needed faster through experimentation
  • No suprises at the end - Discover problems earlier

How? Experiment

  • Make reality visible - Inspect and Adapt
  • Prioiritise learning - Develop a culture of Continuous Improvement
  • Listen - How can you better meet each others' needs?
  • Collaborate - Work together to improve flow
  • Reflect & Support - Be prepared to be wrong
